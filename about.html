Our Story

Founded in 2015, Stride began with a simple mission: to create footwear that combines style, comfort, and durability without compromise.

Every pair is designed with the modern man in mind — versatile enough for work, play, and everything in between.

View Collection

Meet the Founder

James Wellington, the visionary behind Stride, combined his passion for classic style and modern functionality to launch the brand.

His background in industrial design and love for timeless fashion ensures every shoe is not just worn — it's experienced.
